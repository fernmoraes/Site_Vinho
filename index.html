Velvet Symphony

Savor the exquisite harmony of Velvet Symphony, a captivating red blend that weaves together the boldness of ripe blackberries, the subtle sweetness of vanilla, and the refined touch of oak. With its deep ruby hue, this wine is a visual delight, hinting at the sensory journey that awaits.
On the palate, Velvet Symphony unfolds with grace, revealing layers of flavor that dance seamlessly across the taste buds. Dark cherries and plum take center stage, accompanied by whispers of spice and a lingering earthy undertone. Balanced tannins ensure a velvety-smooth texture, while the wine's lingering finish leaves a lasting impression, inviting you to lose yourself in its symphony of flavors time and time again.
